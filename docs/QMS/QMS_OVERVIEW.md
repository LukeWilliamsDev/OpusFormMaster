## 1. Quality Policy Statement

Opus Form Ltd is committed to delivering reliable, safe, and continuously improving concrete placement and formwork services across the UK construction sector. Our internal workforce management portal supports this commitment by:

- Ensuring staff competence through rigorous compliance document tracking (CSCS, certificates, right-to-work)
- Meeting all applicable UK legislation (UK GDPR, CDM Regulations 2015, Health & Safety at Work Act 1974)
- Maintaining robust internal processes for scheduling, quality control, and continual improvement
- Providing clear accountability through comprehensive audit trails and role-based access

This policy applies to all internal business operations managed through the Opus Form portal.

## 3. Plan-Do-Check-Act (PDCA) Cycle

All processes follow the PDCA cycle:

1. **Plan** — Define objectives, identify risks, plan resources
2. **Do** — Execute processes, collect data
3. **Check** — Monitor and measure against objectives, review audit logs
4. **Act** — Implement corrective actions, update processes

## 5. Document Control

- All QMS documents are version-controlled in the Git repository (`docs/QMS/`)
- Changes are tracked via commit history and require review before merge
- The "Last Reviewed" date on each document indicates currency
- Audit logs in the portal provide a supplementary record of operational changes

---

## 6. Management Review

Management reviews should be conducted **quarterly** to assess:

- Service delivery performance metrics
- Compliance document status (overdue certs, expiring CSCS cards)
- Incident log and corrective actions taken
- Client feedback and complaints
- Opportunities for improvement

---

## 7. Continual Improvement

Improvement opportunities are identified through:

- System audit trail analysis (anomalous patterns, error rates)
- Quarterly patch management audits (see `docs/PATCH_LOG.md`)
- Client/staff feedback
- Post-incident reviews
